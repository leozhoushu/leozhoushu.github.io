Future Timber Frame

This program is based on one project done in DADA digital factory 2015. The purpose is to help create a easy-manipulated system by which people can design their own living spaces. The system will calculate the exact shape of all construction components which can be easily machined. In the end, the whole autonomous building phase will be done from bottom to up and conducted efficiently.

Public corridors are occupied @ Longchang Flats, Shanghai

Social Problems —
People in China have limited living space

China is undergoing fast urbanization, more and more people are swarming into the city, making price of housing unaffordable for ordinary residents. People are thus trapped in old apartments and use all kinds of methods to enlarge their living spaces by adding new structure on existing building.

Research — What future construction process will be like?

Accordingly, we made two design decisions:
1) We chose wood as structure material

It is light to handle, and it is not quite expensive for ordinary people. With the help of CNC, people can easily process this material.


2) New design will not be limited by orthogonal frame struture

Users' participation will make the final result irregular. But the advanced machine including robot technology will help make digital models into true architectures.

Fianl Design —
1) Set up a plan

On a grid pattern, there are many circles representing the main columns. People would need to connect a series of circles in a continuous line. The line represents the beam. One red line will match one green line.
 
The potential structure can be either an orthogonal frame or an abnormal one.

2) Set height for each column

People then may set heights for columns. Some space can be high and wide while others can be low and narrow.
 
Within the system, people can also make timber frame as structure for floors or walls or twisted three-dimensional future spaces.

3) Generate unique timber structure

Different generated stuctures can be presented together which is convenient for people to compare and choose the ideal one. Joints of the chosen timber frame will be created by algorithm automaticlly according to angles between each two wood sticks.
 
The digital model of each component will be sent to CNC Factory. With the processed wood, people can build their own design quickly.
